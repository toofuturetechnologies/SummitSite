## Content Guidelines

### SEO Best Practices
- Use H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections
- Include target keyword in title, intro, and H2s
- Aim for 1500-2500 words
- Use alt text for all images
- Include internal links (3-5 per post)
- Meta description: 150-160 characters

### Tone & Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Use "you" when giving advice
- Share genuine experiences
- Avoid hype or exaggeration
- Balance practical info with inspiration

### Images
- Hero image: 1200x630px
- Section images: 600x400px
- All images optimized (<100KB)
- Include diverse adventurers
- Natural lighting preferred

### Publishing Schedule
- 2 posts per week
- Monday & Thursday (peak engagement)
- Share on social media after publication
- Send to email subscribers
